STEAKGRILL® Graphics Manual This Manual defines the basic rules for working with texts and logos of STEAKGRILL® Restaurant. Respective source files for further processing or printing are available for download for each example. All the files are available in the global file on GD.
Colors The basic colors are based on the logo and are extended with so-called secondary colors for the purposes of the pictogram and more. Black and white are included among the basic colors in addition to those specified. No other colors may be used, in accordance with the Manual.

Text styles and font The freely available sans-serif Roboto font is the exclusive font. Plain paragraph style is the basic style. All the other styles are derived from it. No other modifications to the specified styles are permitted, nor are other fonts or other styles as such.

Logo The logo may be used exclusively in the specified logo/background color combinations. When using it, make sure the logo still contrasts with the background, and is legible overall. The logo protective area has to be maintained without exception.

Pictogram The pictogram is defined as the top part of the logo placed in the bottom right corner of the application so that the bottom right section of the circle is not visible. Unlike the logo, the pictogram has to have a low contrast value, so that it does not intrude on the foreground text.

Logo applications Slight modifications to dimensions, including side ratios, are possible in applications (business cards, folders). However, the above rules have to be observed. In particular, the requirement for minimum font size (above) must not be violated. Rounding of corners of printed matter is optional and at least partial rounding is recommended for practical reasons.
